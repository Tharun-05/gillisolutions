Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency service calls?

For emergency service calls, our standard response time is within 4 hours. Premium service plan clients receive priority response, typically within 2 hours. In major metropolitan areas, we often achieve response times under 1 hour.

Do you provide service for all brands of kitchen equipment?

Yes, our technicians are trained to service and repair equipment from all major manufacturers. We maintain relationships with suppliers to ensure we can source parts for virtually any commercial kitchen equipment brand.

What is included in your maintenance service?

Our maintenance service includes thorough inspection, cleaning of critical components, lubrication of moving parts, calibration of temperature controls, safety checks, and performance testing. We also provide a detailed report with recommendations for any necessary repairs or part replacements.

Can you help with kitchen design for a new restaurant?

Absolutely! Our consultation services include kitchen layout design, equipment selection, workflow optimization, and budget planning. We can create 3D visualizations of your kitchen and provide detailed specifications for contractors. Our goal is to design a kitchen that maximizes efficiency while meeting your specific culinary needs.

Do you offer warranties on repairs?

Yes, all our repair work comes with a 90-day warranty on both parts and labor. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no additional cost. For certain major repairs, extended warranties may be available.
