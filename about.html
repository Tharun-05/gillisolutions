About Us

Pioneering Kitchen Solutions

Established 2013

Gilli Solutions, founded in 2013, is company that specialize in manufacturing complete kitchen ventilation system that provide the highest performance, efficiency and capacity for the money. We at Gilli Solutions have highly experienced professionals and have resulted in more than 250+ successful installations across India at very economic prices for the complete satisfaction of customers over last 12+ years.

We design, install and commission kitchen ventilation systems which include exhaust hood, filter, ducts and blower and silencers. Our quality fume ventilation create pleasing atmosphere in kitchen room. Gilli Solutions is proud and committed in maintaining a safe, healthy, and comfortable environment in commercial kitchens.
